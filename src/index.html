Inside the Loom & Leaf Memory Foam Mattress

Every layer of our premium memory foam mattress is designed to give you cool, supportive comfort and durability. We never compromise that quality by compressing and stuffing it into a box.

1. Quilted cover with organic cotton

Soft, breathable, and hypoallergenic, our certified organic cotton is treated with our exclusive Guardin botanical antimicrobial treatment. Quilted with two layers of plush comfort foams and a nontoxic natural thistle flame barrier.

2. Cooling gel-infused foam

Proprietary spinal zone gel layer keeps you cool as it provides maximum spinal support.

3. Ultra-premium 5-lb memory foam

Unlike cheaper bed-in-box foam beds, we use 5-lb, high-density memory foam for lasting support and pressure-point relief.

4. High-density foam base

Multiple layers of support at the base of the mattress give it the durability and stability you can count for a comfortable night's sleep.

Choose your comfort level

Loom & Leaf comes in two firmness options so you can pick one that feels just right for your body.

Relaxed Firm5-7

Our most popular comfort level suits all sleep positions with an ideal balance of plush comfort and contouring support. It allows for healthy spinal alignment whether you sleep on your back, side, or stomach. It's also ideal for couples with different comfort preferences and sleep styles.

Firm8

Ideal for stomach sleepers or anyone who prefers the feel of a very firm mattress. The firmer foams will make you feel like you're floating on top of the mattress rather than sinking into it.

A memory foam mattress that's better for our planet

The eco-friendly foams we use in our Loom & Leaf are made with renewable oils and produce fewer greenhouse gases during manufacturing.

Our nationwide manufacturing network means our mattresses travel less than 100 miles on average from the factory straight to your home, keeping our carbon footprint low.

Bed-in-a-box (BIAB) mattresses are compressed, shrink-wrapped, folded, then rolled into a cardboard box for shipping. While this allows BIAB companies to save on freight costs, it takes a severe toll on the integrity of the foam mattress by breaking down the cell structure of the foams. It can also result in uneven firmness and sinking support in some areas, reducing the overall lifespan of the mattress.

We believe your bed deserves better than being stuffed in a box. Thats why all Saatva mattresses, including our Loom & Leaf Memory Foam Mattress, come with FREE white glove delivery and in-home set up. We even take away your old mattress for free!

Memory foam, also known as viscoelastic foam, is a type of high-density polyurethane foam. Its distinguishing feature is that it softens in response to heat and pressure. Thats what gives memory foam mattresses their body-hugging, body-contouring feel.

The durability of a foam mattress can vary greatly depending on the quality and density of the foams. A premium, high-quality memory foam mattress, like our Loom & Leaf, should last you anywhere from 10 to 15 years.

We only use nontoxic CertiPUR-US certified foams in our mattresses. This means that the foams are made without CFCs and other ozone depleters. They also dont use PBDEs, TDCPP, or TCEP ("Tris"), which are flame retardants that have been identified by the Globally Harmonized System (GHS) as substances that may cause cancer, genetic defects, or damage fertility.

CertiPUR-US compliant foams are free of phthalates, formaldehyde, and mercury, lead, or other heavy metals. They're also tested to verify low VOC (volatile organic compound) emissions, so your mattress wont off-gas in your home.

This type of foam material is infused with a cooling gel to ensure a cool and comfortable sleeping environment. Our Loom & Leaf premium memory foam mattress has the added feature of a proprietary spinal zone gel layer that keeps you cool as it provides enhanced spinal support.

Foams that are CertiPUR-US certified are guaranteed to be low in volatile organic compounds (VOCs), so you dont have to worry about off-gassing odors. CertiPUR-US certified foams are also guaranteed to be free of ozone depleters, PBDEs, TDCPP, and TCEP flame retardants, mercury, lead, and other heavy metals, formaldehyde, and phthalates.

Our exclusive antimicrobial treatment by Guardin is made with a proprietary blend of all-natural essential oils and minerals. This blend includes thyme, geranium, lemongrass, and mint, but is completely odorless and colorless.

It naturally inhibits the growth of bacteria, mold, mildew, and the discoloration that they can cause on the surface of a mattress. Guardin antimicrobial treatment is biodegradable and does not contain any fluorocarbons, toxic heavy metals, silver nanotechnology, triclosan, formaldehyde or phthalates.
